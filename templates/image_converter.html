Image Format Comparison

Understanding different image formats helps you choose the right one:

  • JPG/JPEG - Best for photographs, supports compression
  • PNG - Best for graphics with transparency, lossless compression
  • GIF - Supports animation, limited to 256 colors
  • WEBP - Modern format, excellent compression, supports animation
  • SVG - Vector format, scalable without quality loss
  • BMP - Uncompressed format, large file sizes
  • TIFF - High quality, preferred for printing
